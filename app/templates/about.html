About Women Safety App

The Women Safety App is designed to protect and empower women in emergency situations. This application allows users to quickly connect with trusted contacts and emergency services with a single tap.

Features such as instant alerts, emergency contact access, and location sharing ensure timely help and safety anytime, anywhere.
